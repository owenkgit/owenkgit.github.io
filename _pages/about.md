## About Me

I graduated with an **MS from Carnegie Mellon University**, focusing on robotics under the guidance of **Prof. Jean Oh** and **Prof. Amir Barati Farimani**.

### Research Focus

My research addresses **rapid adaptation of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models** to new environments with minimal resources. I explore how natural language instructionsâ€”ranging from coarse directives like "be safe" to specific commands like "move 20% slower"â€”translate into physical action corrections through post-training alignment.

Key areas include:
- **Skill-based action tokenizers** for stable long-horizon control
- **Retrieval frameworks** that autonomously gather robot data with minimal supervision
- **Test-time adaptation** of robotic systems through human feedback

## Overview
Hands-on course focused on designing, implementing, and analyzing AI-model infrastructure using large real-world datasets with modern ML design frameworks.

## Focus Areas
- **Data Engineering**: Efficient collection, storage, and streaming of large datasets
- **AI Infrastructure**: Scalable infrastructure for large-scale model training and deployment
- **Data Quality**: Maintaining high data quality and integrity throughout the pipeline

## Key Challenges
- Handling and processing large volumes of real-world data efficiently
- Ensuring infrastructure scalability to accommodate growing data and model complexity
- Maintaining data quality and integrity throughout the data engineering process

## Technologies
Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Data Engineering, Data Collection, Data Storage, Data Streaming, ML Design Frameworks, Large Datasets, AI Infrastructure
